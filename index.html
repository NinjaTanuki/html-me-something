Berserk!

"WARNING! this is one if not the most brutal and depressing stories I have read.While i believe the contents of this manga is presented tastefully, this manga is not for the light of heart or those easily triggered by depictions of rape, murder, gore, and other similar themes. You have been warned.

"This manga follows the story of Gut's a wondering mercenary as he overcomes major hardships on a quest for Survival. After abandoning the mercenary group that initaly took him in heacts as a lone wolf, striking down all those who would be unfourtunate enough to meet his path. This all changes on day when he is discovered by a man name griffith who recruits the reluctant Guts into his Mercenary group The band of hawks.The two soon form a strong bond in pursuit of their goals, only to be met With tragedy after tragedy. Will they ever reach their goals? or will the seemingly impossiable to overcome obsticals be their ultimate demise?"

Hopefully I did not the give to much away in that summary! I stubbled across this when I was about 12 years old and while I was enamored with its amazing art and graphic almost gut wrenching depictions, I could hardly wrap my head around such a morbid story.Fortunatly over the years I have come to recognize what the writer is truely trying to convey in a story fully a tragedy.Its a must read for any fan of japanese manga or anime.It updates bi monthly and is still going strong with 351 chapters

"Shi-Woon yi is a young highschool student who is fed up with being bullied by his fellow classmates. After discovering the new subsitiute teacher,Chun-Woo Han, is a martial artist he begs for the teacher to instruct him how to properly defend himself.though reluctant at first Shi-woon and his teacher soon form a strong bond... but at what cost! Chun-woo has now introduced Shi-woon into the world of Murim, a underground society of brutal martial artist. Fortunatly for him his teacher is the best there is! will he be able to survive in such a world?>"

This one is technically cheating as it is not a manga but a manwha, a Korean graphic novel, BUT! it is by far the best martial arts manga i have read. It is Filled with amazing semi over the top action scenes and a story that has you fully invested in its characters and their progression. this story is broken up into 2 parts at the moment. The Breaker and The Breaker: New Waves. Unfourtunaly the story is complete and Part 3 has yet to be annouced since the ending of New Waves in 2014. Even so This is still Very much worth the read finished or not!

The only force stoping the ghouls form wreaking compleet havok on civilian populace is the CCG(Commision of Counter Ghouls).We hollow Ken Kaneki a quite unssepecting colleged student, who's first date Truns out to be a ghoul herself. After narrowly survivng the encounter Kaneki soon discovers he soon discovers he is a half ghoul as a result of the surgery.After gaining allies in an attempt to leave a peaceful new life He is pushed into the ongoing conflict between the ghouls and the CCG, will he be able to survive?"

This manga is among the best to be published in the last 5 years. Unfoutunalty this manga is another psycological tragedy full of mystery and horror elements boasting amazing visuals. It offers light escapeism for the reader to connect and grow along with the main charcter routing for him to succeed. However with every push forward he always seems to find himself 2 step back yet again. Even with this being the case the writer has still found away to keep the story enticing enough for you wanting more with is unquie supporting chracter and antagonist.The managa has two parts Tokyo Ghoul and Tokyo Ghoul:Re the latter still ongoing. Again anothe must read if not for the art alone.

Full Metal Alchmist: Brotherhood

"Taking place in a alternate timeline in the fictional country of Amestris. In this world almechy is the most practiced science as those who master the science through the aid of transmutation circles can creat almost anything.However the most important law is alchemy is the Law of Equvilant exchange. We follow the Elric brothers, Edward, who is missing his right arm and left leg, and Alphonse, who's soul is bound to a suit of armor, after failing to ressurct their deceased mother with the forbidden alchemic art called Human Transmutation. Edward although taken aback by his sins, becomes a state alchemist after recovering and now embarks on a journey around the country with his brother Alphonse to find a way to get their bodies back!"

This anime falls very close to my heart makes is considered a must watch by many anime fans. Infact I would go as far to say you are not a true fan if you have not atleast watched a few epsiode of this amazing anime.There are two versions The orginal 2006 full metal alchmeist anime which is an atlernate telling of the sotry and the 2009 brotherhood anime which stays true to its source material. The 2009 anime is conseried far superior as its animation is top notch and its paceing is spot on compared to the 2006 version.Both are about 50 epsidoes long and no matter which one you choose to watch both will have you wanting more by the end.Fourtunalty both verision have movies that take place during and after the ending of the show.

Bleach

"Ichigo Kurosaki is you adverage everyday highschool student... except for the fact that he can see the Spirits!! while this dosent cause him much concern since hes been able to see them since he was young that changes one day when his family is attacked by a corrupt spirit called a hollow. While he initially stands no chance against the beast the tables are turned when the local Soul Reaper (A death god) Rukia who is injured while protecting Ichigo imbues him with her soul reaper powers! With the monster now defeated it is revealed that rukia cannot regain her powers, leaving Ichigo the task of protecting the town against any hollow threat that should arise! "

Bleach is rather long boasting a whopping 365 episodes and while at its introduction was apart of the big 3 anime ( naruto and one peice) that took off in popularity among western and japanese audiences is now considered mediocre at best.and while that may be true i consider it one of the most action animes ever produced. While it may seem a bit shallow at first the story gradually picks up as it explores concepts of death, sacrifice, as the main character Ichigo fights to protect his family friends.
